Church Directory

Eliminate that dated printed directory and cut calls to your church office by making your member directory available on the web!

All access is secured by unique log-in using each member's personal email address.

Profiles

Give your members a place to upload their own photo, personal testimony, contact info, and more.

Groups

Groups connect people in Sunday School classes, Small Groups, or special projects. Use OneBody Groups for classes, special events, trip planning, elder/staff and leadership communication, or anywhere you might use an email mailing list.

Attendance

Keep records of members attendance and back it up to the cloud.

What's Bethel?

Bethel App is a port of the awesome FREE AND OPEN SOURCE SOFTWARE, OneBody by CHURCH.IO. Church.IO is a community of developers, designers, and users. We believe church software is too expensive and difficult to use, and we're working to change that by creating awesome open source church software.
